## ðŸ’ª Exercise Protocols (Weeks 1-4 Focus)

### Phase 1: Neuromuscular Isolation

#### 1. Transverse Abdominis Activation
**Position:** Supine, knees bent
**Technique:**
- Draw belly button to spine without breath-holding
- Hold 5 seconds, release slowly
- 10 reps x 3 sets
**Goal:** Create internal "corset" support

#### 2. Modified Crocodile Breathing
**Position:** Prone with towel under right foot
**Technique:**
- Inhale 4 counts into posterior ribs
- Focus gentle left-side expansion
- Exhale with right foot dorsiflexion
- 3-5 minutes daily
**Integration:** Links foot to rib via Spiral Line

#### 3. Quadratus Lumborum Release
**Position:** Side-lying on uninjured side
**Technique:**
- Foam roll from left hip to ribs
- Pause at tender spots (30 seconds)
- Add contralateral arm reach
- 2 minutes per side
**Caution:** Avoid excessive pressure at L1-L2

#### 4. Wall Angel Progression
**Position:** Back against wall
**Technique:**

- Sharp, stabbing pain at suture sites
- Sudden breathlessness or chest tightness
- Numbness/tingling in ribs or abdomen
- Fever or signs of infection
- Pain >7/10 lasting >30 minutes
